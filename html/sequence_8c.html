◆ SEQUENCE_PAR_BITS

#define SEQUENCE_PAR_BITS   (24U)

Parallel bit generation for x1/x2 sequences parameters. Exploits the fact that the sequence generation is 31 chips ahead and the maximum register shift is 3 (for x2). The maximum number of parallel bits is 28, 16 is optimal for SSE.
