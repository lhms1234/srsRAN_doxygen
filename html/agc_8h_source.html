49 /*
50  * The AGC has been implemented using 3 states:
51  * - init: it simply starts the process of measuring
52  * - measure: performs a minimum of SRSRAN_AGC_MIN_MEASUREMENTS and does not set the gain until it needs
53  * SRSRAN_AGC_MIN_GAIN_OFFSET dB more of gain. The gain is set in the enter hold transition.
54  * - hold: waits for SRSRAN_AGC_HOLD_COUNT frames as a Rx gain transition period. After this period, it enters measure
55  * state.
56  *
57  * FSM abstraction:
58  *
59  * +------+ Enter measure +---------+ Enter hold +------+
60  * | init | ---------------->| Measure |--------------->| Hold |
61  * +------+ +---------+ +------+
62  * ^ Enter measure |
63  * +-------------------------+
64  */
