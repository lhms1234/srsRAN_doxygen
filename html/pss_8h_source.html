22 /******************************************************************************
23  * File: pss.h
24  *
25  * Description: Primary synchronization signal (PSS) generation and detection.
26  *
27  * The srsran_pss_t object provides functions for fast
28  * computation of the crosscorrelation between the PSS and received
29  * signal and CFO estimation. Also, the function srsran_pss_tperiodic()
30  * is designed to be called periodically every subframe, taking
31  * care of the correct data alignment with respect to the PSS sequence.
32  *
33  * The object is designed to work with signals sampled at 1.92 Mhz
34  * centered at the carrier frequency. Thus, downsampling is required
35  * if the signal is sampled at higher frequencies.
36  *
37  * Reference: 3GPP TS 36.211 version 10.0.0 Release 10 Sec. 6.11.1
38  *****************************************************************************/
