22 /******************************************************************************
23  * File: sync.h
24  *
25  * Description: Time and frequency synchronization using the PSS and SSS signals.
26  *
27  * The object is designed to work with signals sampled at 1.92 Mhz
28  * centered at the carrier frequency. Thus, downsampling is required
29  * if the signal is sampled at higher frequencies.
30  *
31  * Correlation peak is detected comparing the maximum at the output
32  * of the correlator with a threshold. The comparison accepts two
33  * modes: absolute value or peak-to-mean ratio, which are configured
34  * with the functions sync_pss_det_absolute() and sync_pss_det_peakmean().
35  *
36  *
37  * Reference: 3GPP TS 36.211 version 10.0.0 Release 10 Sec. 6.11.1, 6.11.2
38  *****************************************************************************/
